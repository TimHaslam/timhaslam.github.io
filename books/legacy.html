Chapter 2 - Adapt

  • Four Stages of Organizational Change
    1. A Case for Change
    2. A Compelling Picture of the Future
    3. A Sustained Ability to Change
    4. A Credible Plan to Execute
  • Decision Cycle or OODA Loop
    • Observe
    • Orient
    • Decide
    • Act
  • Boyd (creater of OODA Loop) studied the results of dogfights in the Korean War. The pilots who got into the OODA loop were the ones that survived.
  • It is not the strongest species that survive. Nor the most intelligent, but the ones most responsive to change.
