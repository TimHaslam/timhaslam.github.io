California Arcadia Mission - August 4, 2004 to June 18, 2006

  • Mission Call

    May 5, 2004

    I received my mission call on May 5, 2004. I remember it well for two reasons. 1) I had my wisdom teeth pulled that morning. 2) It was Cinco de Mayo. I went to a party that night at Mark Lowes house and the medication from the wisdom teeth surgery made me sick so I was throwing up in the bushes. It was just my parents and I in the basement of Glen Oaks when I opened it. I remember being really excited. I also remember that a few months earlier, we had gone to visit the Mazurans in Alhambra, California and we saw the missionaries, specifically Elder Brinkerhoff. I told them I was turning in my papers and he said, "It would be crazy if you called here." His prophecy was correct!
