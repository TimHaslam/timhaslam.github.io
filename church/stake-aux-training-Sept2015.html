Elevating the Young Men Through Effective Quorum Meetings

Tonight we would like to talk about the importance of A) having regular quorum presidency meetings and B) making sure they elevate the young men and build capability.

Handbook

In Church handbooks, the term quorum leaders refers to the deacons quorum presidency, the teachers quorum presidency, and the bishop and his assistants in the priests quorum. The members of the Young Men presidency are quorum advisers, not quorum leaders.

Effective Aaronic Priesthood Presidency and Quorum Meetings

Notes

1. Don't have direction without presidency meetings. How many of you are regularly communicating with the quorum presidency? Imagine if the bishopric didn't meet weekly?

2. Gives an opportunity to review assignments and discuss the needs of the quorum. (District Meeting)

3. Makes them prepared for class.

4. Leaders get to train and provide training for the young men. (District Meeting)

5. Teach them their duty so they can actually do it. If we don't teach them, we can't expect them to do it.

6. Helps build quorum strength and unity.

7. Helps build each member individually as well.

8. Agendas are important! - Helps focus, determine needs. Agendas enable the young men to be effective leaders. Allows them to exercise their priesthood without having to counsel since that's already happened.

9. If we are prepared, the Spirt of the Lord will come into that meeting.
