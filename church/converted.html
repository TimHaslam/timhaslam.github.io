Come Follow Me: How do I know if I'm becoming converted?

Scriptures

  1. Enos 1:1-19, 26-27 - Remembered the words of his father, lose desire to sin, filled with love, share gospel with others
  2. Mosiah 5:1-5, 27:23-37 - Lose desire to sin, do good continually, covenant with God.
  3. Alma 23:6-7 - Christ must be the center of our conversion
  4. Helaman 3:35 - Purify heart, yield it to God
  5. 4 Nephi 1:1-4, 15 - No contention, love of God in their hearts
  6. Luke 22:32 - Strengthen thy brethren
  7. Matt 25:1-13 - Parable of the 10 virgins
  8. Alma 23:7 - Lay down your weapons of rebellion. Bednar - "Do you and I still possess weapons of rebellion that keep us from becoming converted unto the Lord?"

Activity

  • Show examples of things that change over time. Seeds to plants, tadpoles to frogs, etc.
  • Create a list of questions that we can ask ourselves to evaluate our conversion.

Quotes

David A Bednar, 2013 October General Conference, 'Converted unto the Lord'

Testimony: ...a gift from God and is available to all of His children.
Conversion requires a permanent change in our very nature made possible through the Atonement. Conversion brings a change in one's beliefs, heart and life to accept and conform to the will of God.

Bonnie Oscarson, 2012 October General Conference, 'Be ye converted'

It is possible to have a testimony of the gospel without living it.
...we are often guilty of expecting the reward without having to work for it.
