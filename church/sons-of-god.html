Sons of God v. Sons of Man

  1. Ether 3:11-15 - Brother of Jared became Son of God and saw God. v. 15 - Was this the first time someone saw God? (NO!)
  2. D&C 107:53-55 - Adam, Seth, Enos, Cainan, Mahaleleel, Jared, Enoch, Methuselah all saw God. Why? Because they were Sons of God.
  3. Moses 7:4 - Moses saw God
  4. 3 Nephi 9:17 - Receive & believe in Christ to become a Son of God (Also John 1:12)
  5. D&C 11:30 - Receive Christ & get Priesthood power to become a Son of God.
  6. Moroni 7:45-48 - Must have charity to be a Son of God
  7. 1 John 2:28-29 - Abide in Him, have confidence, not be ashamed
  8. 1 John 4:7 - Love one another
  9. 1 John 5:1 - Love Christ
  10. Galatians 3:26-27 - Faith & Baptism
  11. Mosiah 5:5-9 - Enter into covenants, obey the commandments, change of heart through faith, take upon us the name of Christ, ETTE
  12. D&C 34:3 - Believe
  13. D&C 45:8 - Believe, perform miracles through Priesthood power
  14. Moses 7:1 - Believe, be saved from sin, comforted
  15. Moses 6:64-68 - Adam was the model to become a Son of God. Wasn't a Son of God until he received the Melchizedek Priesthood.
